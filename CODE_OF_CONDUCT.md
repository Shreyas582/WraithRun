# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, harassment-free community.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while reviewing technical changes.
- Focus criticism on ideas, not people.
- Do not engage in harassment, discrimination, or abuse.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate
this policy, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
necessary to keep the community safe.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security/contact issue or
contact the maintainers directly.
